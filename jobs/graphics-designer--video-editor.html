Graphics Designer and Video Editor

In a Nutshell:

The Solutionist is looking for a Graphic Design Executive who can create beautiful and intuitive user experiences. The core responsibilities for this role include developing visuals and motion graphics for e-commerce and social media platforms. The candidates must have a keen eye for clean and artful design, possess superior UI skills, and be able to translate high-level requirements into interaction flows and functional user interfaces. In this role, you will also be working closely with our product teams, users, and development team in an agile and interactive design-focused environment.

Once you are here, you will:

Social media ads and banners:

  • Create Google and Facebook ads keeping the brand guidelines in mind
  • Make adaptations of ads in different dimensions 
  • Having knowledge about Facebook ads variations
  • Create social media cover banners for every new launch
  • Be up to date with policies and optimal sizes for graphics on social media platforms
  • Contribute to the advancement of the team’s design methodology by focusing on quality, content performance, and efficiency
  • Closely coordinate with other marketing team members
  • Exploring new ideas for the ads and web banners

E-commerce website: 

  • Develop visuals for e-commerce as per requirements from marketing teams
  • Design website main and category page banners and optimize them 
  • Create website blog banners and GIFs
  • Create website pop-ups banner designs
  • Perform website product photos retouch, masking, and optimization

Create Insta stories and motion graphics

  • Create product-specific Instagram stories using product images and text animation
  • Create interactive stories with call-to-action animation
  • Bring new concepts and ideas for the stories
  • Ensure consistency of design in all stories

Email Marketing:

  • Create email templates keeping the brand guidelines in mind
  • Create GIFs for hero images and optimize it
  • Bring new ideas and concepts for different email marketing campaigns

Other Responsibilities:

  • Keep a track of the tasks and have knowledge about Trello workflow
  • Graphics developed should depict the brand alignment and be engaging for the audience at the same time
  • Keeping up-to-date with new developments in relevant design, technology, standards, and standard methodologies

 

What does The Solutionist offer you?

The Solutionist is a new age technology company that is focused on just one thing: keeping you relevant in the market. We work hard. Harder than most other places of work in the same hours. But we also purposefully find ways to allow our teams to enjoy work together. As a result, we’re among the best companies to work for in Pakistan with a plethora of benefits and an amazing culture.

Relevant Experience:

  • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration with Marketing Majors of equivalent
  • At least 1 - 2 years of experience in designing content for Websites, Social media, or E-Commerce
  • Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and After-Effects

Remote Work During COVID19 Pandemic

  • UPS for Critical Staff
  • Monthly Internet Allowance

Additionally, our Admin team is on standby at all times to help Gaditians in any way they can. Finally, a detailed WFH policy and guide to enable productive work from home for all teams are available.
